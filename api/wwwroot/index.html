Payments in Lesotho are fragmented

Mobile money rails such as EcoCash and M‑Pesa are convenient for day‑to‑day payments and have become the entry point to the financial system for many Basotho【647492880502077†L315-L324】. At the other end, bank EFT systems handle salaries and large transfers but are slower, rigid and rely on limited cut‑off times【647492880502077†L326-L340】. Cross‑border remittances remain expensive and slow, often requiring customers to cash out and re‑enter funds into another system【647492880502077†L332-L373】.

This fragmentation forces developers to integrate multiple systems that speak different dialects and have wildly different settlement times. MonoPay abstracts the complexity by acting as a gateway between all rails. We deliver a single, consistent API so you can focus on building your product.
